Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to reach out to our support team.

What exactly is shared web hosting?

Shared hosting is a type of web hosting where multiple websites reside on a single web server connected to the internet. This is the most economical option for hosting, as the overall cost of server maintenance is amortized over many customers.

Are SSL certificates included?

Yes! We provide free Let's Encrypt SSL certificates with all our hosting plans. They are automatically installed and renewed, ensuring your website remains secure without any manual intervention.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your hosting plan at any time right from your client dashboard. The transition is seamless, and you will only be charged the prorated difference.

Do you offer website migrations?

Yes, our expert migration team will move your existing website to Vexron Hosting completely free of charge. Simply open a support ticket after purchasing your plan, and we'll handle the rest with zero downtime.
